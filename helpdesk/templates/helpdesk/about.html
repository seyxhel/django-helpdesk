About PrimeDesk Technologies

PrimeDesk Technologies builds and supports a modern, extensible helpdesk platform based on the proven django-helpdesk project. Our focus is on delivering a simple user experience for support teams while providing the configurability and integrations necessary for production deployments.

Overview

The platform provides ticket-based customer support with flexible queue management, assignment and escalation rules, automated email handling, and customizable templates. It scales from small teams to multi-tenant environments and exposes APIs and webhooks for automation and integrations.

Key features

  • Ticket queues & routing — Organize incoming issues into queues and route them automatically to the right teams or agents.
  • Email integration — Inbound and outbound email handling with templates, threading, and attachments.
  • Permissions & teams — Fine-grained staff roles, per-queue permissions, and shared inbox workflows.
  • Automation — Saved replies, canned responses, SLA rules, and queue-specific behaviors to reduce manual work.
  • Reporting & analytics — Built-in reports and exportable metrics for response time, backlog, and agent performance.
  • Extensibility — REST API, webhooks, and template overrides for custom integrations and UI changes.

Security & reliability

The system supports secure authentication, role-based access control, and configurable logging. Deployments commonly run behind HTTPS and integrate with existing identity providers and monitoring systems.

Deployment & integrations

PrimeDesk is deployable as a standalone Django application or alongside existing services. Common integrations include SMTP/IMAP email, SSO providers, external ticketing or CRM systems, and chat platforms via webhooks.

Getting started

To evaluate PrimeDesk, install the demo or follow the documentation to connect your mailboxes and create your first queue. Configure staff accounts, saved replies, and a basic workflow to see immediate value.

Community & support
